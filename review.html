WHAT TO WATCH

COMEDY

Silicon Valley

A comedy series from HBO that started in the Spring of 2014 and is currently in its 4th season. The series revolves around 6 young men who founded a company in Silicon Valley.

Rating: ☆ ☆ ☆

Airs on Sundays at 10pm EST.

DRAMA

Power

An American crime drama series from Starz which premiered in the Summer of 2014 and is currently in its 4th season. Created by rapper "50 Cent", the series follows a man trying to balance his life between being a nightclub owner and major player in the illegal drug world.

Rating: ☆ ☆ ☆

Airs on Sundays at 9pm EST.

ACTION

Game of Thrones

An American fantasy drama series from HBO which premiered in the Spring of 2011 and is currently in its 7th season. Game of Thrones has multiple plot lines and is based on a series of novels by George R. R. Martin. It is incredibly well produced, not only from the story line, but visually, and worth the time investment of watching the series from the beginning.

Rating: ☆ ☆ ☆ ☆ ☆

Airs on Sundays at 9pm EST.

REALITY

Alaskan Bush People

An American reality series from Discover Channel which premiered in the Spring of 2014 and is currently in its 7th season. The show follows the life of a family living in the Alaskan wildnerness.

Rating: ☆ ☆

Airs on Wednesdays at 7pm EST.

CURRENT EVENTS

VICE: News Tonight

An American news program from HBO which premiered in the Fall of 2016 and is currently in its 1st season. Provides a synopsis of current events occurring nationally and globally, ranging from a variety of topics, such as technology, economics, environment, etc.

Rating: ☆ ☆ ☆ ☆

Airs Monday through Thursday at 7:30pm EST.
